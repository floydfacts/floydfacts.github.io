Consent

Good help - ethical and effective help - requires consent: the helped person must agree to be helped. True consent requires understanding: the helping person must fully inform the helped person about the benefits, limits, and risks of help. Modern law terms this formula informed consent. Good help requires informed consent. The following sections describe the benefits, limits,and risks of the help that I offer.
